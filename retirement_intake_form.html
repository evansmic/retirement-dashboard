💡 Google Sheets Recommended as Your Data Backend

Because you plan to share this with friends and family — and not everyone has Excel — we recommend Google Sheets as your data backend:

  • Free for everyone with a Google account
  • Each person can make their own private copy (File → Make a copy)
  • Works on any device — phone, tablet, laptop — no software needed
  • Your HTML dashboard can read data directly from a published Google Sheet
  • All data stays in each person's own private Google account

This form saves your answers locally in your browser. When you finish, use Export → Google Sheets CSV to get a file you can paste into Google Sheets, or Export → JSON to load into the dashboard directly.

What-If Scenarios & Additional Notes

Define the planning scenarios you want modeled in your retirement dashboard.

🔀 Planning Scenarios

Scenario 1 — Baseline (Auto-Generated)

No active tax planning. CPP and OAS taken at age 65. RRSP/RRIF minimum withdrawals only. This is the comparison baseline — your dashboard will always generate this automatically.

Scenario 2 — Net 0% Rate of Return (Auto-Generated)

All investments earn 0% real return (only inflation kept). Tests the absolute floor of your plan. Auto-generated in your dashboard.

Scenario 3 — RRSP Meltdown + Delayed CPP/OAS (Auto-Generated)

Aggressive RRSP drawdown filling lower tax brackets; CPP and OAS deferred to age 70. The dashboard's signature tax-optimised scenario — auto-generated.
