Minimum monthly expenses

Enter the essential monthly amount you want the plan to test first. Mortgage or debt payments already entered in Debts should stay there.

Minimum expenses are used as the floor for the capacity estimate. They are not treated as the amount you want to spend.

Optional spending assumptions

Lifestyle spending, travel, gifts, and age-based changes can be reviewed later as assumptions. They should not block the first capacity estimate.
