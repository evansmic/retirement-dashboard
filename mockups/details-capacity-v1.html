Why these ages are shown

Ages 65, 72, 82, and 95 are checkpoints for benefit timing, required registered withdrawals, later-retirement spending assumptions, and projection end. A live version should let these assumptions be reviewed.

COLA and inflation treatment

Income, expenses, and benefits can use different indexing assumptions. This mockup should show those assumptions before users rely on the annual rows.
