# Future Account Optimizer Readiness

## Purpose

Keep account-optimizer readiness moving while schema reset planning continues.

This does not implement annual account-level sequencing.

## Readiness Items

### Floor-First Objective

The optimizer should test whether minimum expenses are covered before comparing discretionary room.

### Capacity Objective

The optimizer should estimate confident monthly after-tax capacity from assets, income, tax, and assumptions.

### Funding Trace Contract

The future answer should explain where money appears to come from without turning into account instructions too early.

### Annual Account-Level Sequencing

Annual account-level instructions remain deferred until sequencing readiness is complete.

## Boundary

No optimizer behavior, engine output schema, saved schema, or annual account-level sequencing changes in this package.
