# Sprint 703 - Spending Intake Reframe

## Goal

Reduce the sense that users must know their desired retirement spending before the app can help.

## Completed

- Reframed the intake spending section as regular spending assumptions.
- Directed users to start with regular after-tax expenses in today's dollars.
- Clarified that mortgage payments already entered in Debts should be excluded.
- Renamed phase labels to early, later, and late-life spending.

## Boundary

The underlying `gogo`, `slowgo`, and `nogo` fields remain unchanged for compatibility.
