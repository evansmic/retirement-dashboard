S1769 Mockup Non-Advisory Boundary

Scope
- Keep the Overview mockup consumer-facing and non-advisory.

Guardrails
- Use "appears to come from" for broad funding groups.
- Avoid account-by-account instructions.
- Keep review items neutral.
- Avoid language that sounds like permission to spend.

Result
- The mockup presents a modelled estimate and review context, not a financial recommendation.
