# Sprint 653: Annual Sequencing Decision Criteria

## Purpose
Set a high bar before annual account-level sequencing can be reconsidered.

## Required Signals
- Reviewers understand that broad withdrawal families are comparison evidence, not instructions.
- Reviewers can find the evidence without moderator rescue.
- Tax and OAS recovery evidence is understandable enough to support trust.
- Confusion does not cluster around "which account exactly, in which year."
- No material privacy concern appears in local save, CSV, or report workflow reactions.

## Hold Conditions
- Any reviewer treats broad-family evidence as a direct annual drawdown instruction.
- Reviewers consistently cannot find or interpret the evidence.
- Reviewers ask for advisor approval, cloud sharing, or tax filing outputs as prerequisites.

## Boundary
This sprint does not change the annual sequencing blocker state by itself.
