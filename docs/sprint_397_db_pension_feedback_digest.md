# Sprint 397 - DB Pension Couple Feedback Digest

## Purpose

Capture the first Robert and Margaret DB-pension-couple review as optimizer readiness evidence.

## Feedback captured

- The Overview answer reads as a clear recommended plan rather than a list of options.
- The memorable number is the after-tax monthly spending capacity, in today's dollars.
- Details reads as something to review, not a command or personalized advice.
- Funded years and success evidence matter first, followed by projected money left.
- Tax and OAS recovery are useful diagnostics, but should stay secondary to viability and estate trade-offs.
- Cash-wedge language helps when it describes a buffer, but can become too directive if it tells users when or how to refill cash.

## Boundary

This sprint records feedback only. It does not add annual account-level sequencing, saved output fields, advice language, cloud workflows, or advisor tooling.
