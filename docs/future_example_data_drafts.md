# Future Example Data Drafts

## Purpose

Draft future new-format example values before changing current example plans.

These are planning values only. They are not `.plan.json` files and they are not wired into the app.

## Single Person With A Covered Floor

Household: single renter or mortgage-free owner with moderate registered savings and CPP/OAS income.

Draft values:

- Minimum monthly expenses excluding mortgage: `$3,600`.
- Mortgage monthly payment: `$0`.
- Early spending change age: `75`.
- Later spending change age: `85`.
- Expected capacity status: `covered`.

Review focus:

- Monthly capacity is visible.
- Floor coverage is easy to understand.
- Funding trace stays non-instructional.

Must avoid:

- migrated desired-spending values,
- safe-spend language,
- account withdrawal instructions.

## Couple With A Tight Or Uncovered Floor

Household: retiring couple with moderate savings, a remaining mortgage, and a minimum expense floor that may not be fully covered.

Draft values:

- Minimum monthly expenses excluding mortgage: `$6,200`.
- Mortgage monthly payment: `$1,800`.
- Early spending change age: `74`.
- Later spending change age: `84`.
- Expected capacity status: `gap`.

Review focus:

- Gap options are practical.
- Work-longer and downsize comparisons are not pushy.
- Tax review is visible without advice.

Must avoid:

- single-option pressure,
- automatic recommendation to cut spending,
- false certainty that one option fixes the plan.
