# Sprint 410 - Non-Advisory Example Feedback Guard

## Purpose

Keep richer example feedback from drifting into advice or account instructions.

## Completed

- Confirmed feedback closeout language stays review-only.
- Confirmed the feedback package and withdrawal feedback review are not saved into example plan files.
- Confirmed annual sequencing remains a deferred feedback-package item.

## Boundary

The app may show evidence for review. It must not tell users what to withdraw, when to withdraw it, or treat a bundled example as a personal recommendation.
