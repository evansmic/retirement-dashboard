# Sprint 418 - Spending Range Copy Guard

## Purpose

Prevent spending-range language from sounding like permission or advice.

## Completed

- Guarded against copy that tells users to spend a range.
- Guarded against copy that tells users to follow a guardrail.
- Kept spending ranges as feedback language for review.

## Boundary

Spending flexibility remains explanatory. It must not become an instruction to spend, reduce spending, or change accounts.
