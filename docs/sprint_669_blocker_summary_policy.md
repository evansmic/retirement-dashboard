# Sprint 669: Blocker Summary Policy

## Purpose
Keep blocker rendering focused on the highest-signal issues.

## Policy
- Show a short blocker list in the research panel.
- Prefer performance, scope, and prototype-readiness blockers because they explain why annual sequencing is still deferred.
- Keep detailed blocker clearance prompts in runtime/docs until a real decision packet is needed.

## Boundary
This sprint does not clear blockers, request approval, or start implementation.
