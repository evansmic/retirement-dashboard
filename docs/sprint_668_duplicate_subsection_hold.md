# Sprint 668: Duplicate Subsection Hold

## Purpose
Mark repeated readiness subsections as held from visible rendering unless they reduce reviewer confusion.

## Outcome
- Older detailed readiness subsections remain available as runtime/doc evidence.
- They should not return to the rendered research panel by default.
- Future additions should replace or summarize existing material instead of adding another visible block.

## Boundary
This sprint does not remove test coverage or change optimizer behavior.
