# Sprint 645: Session Script

## Purpose
Provide a calm outside-app session script for broad withdrawal-family evidence review.

## Script
1. Ask the reviewer to open a built-in example or their own local plan.
2. Ask what first answers they notice in Overview.
3. Ask whether the spending and confidence language feels understandable.
4. Ask them to open Details and find the plan review evidence.
5. Ask what they think the broad withdrawal-family comparison is saying.
6. Ask what would make the evidence clearer without asking for annual account instructions.
7. Close by asking whether the product felt private, calm, and non-advisory.

## Moderator Reminders
- Do not coach the reviewer toward a desired answer.
- Do not present the broad family result as a recommendation.
- Do not ask the reviewer to share or upload their plan file.

## Boundary
This sprint does not add product copy, product controls, analytics, or feedback persistence.
