# Sprint 652: Feedback Synthesis Rubric

## Purpose
Define how outside-app feedback should be summarized after three to five reviews.

## Rubric
- **Clear enough for cleanup:** most reviewers can explain the broad-family evidence in their own words.
- **Needs wording cleanup:** reviewers understand the result only after a phrase or label is clarified.
- **Needs organization cleanup:** reviewers lose the evidence in Details even when the evidence itself is understandable.
- **Needs evidence cleanup:** reviewers ask for context that can be supplied with existing runtime evidence.
- **Not ready for sequencing:** reviewers ask for annual account instructions or misunderstand the broad-family boundary.

## Outcome
- Synthesis can separate small cleanup from deferred product scope.
- Annual sequencing remains blocked unless clarity, evidence, and boundary signals are all strong.

## Boundary
This sprint does not summarize real feedback, implement fixes, or approve annual sequencing.
