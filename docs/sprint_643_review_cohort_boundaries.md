# Sprint 643: Review Cohort Boundaries

## Purpose
Set simple boundaries for who should review the current broad withdrawal-family evidence.

## Outcome
- Prefer household profiles that resemble Canadian/Ontario retirement planning use cases.
- Include at least one single-person plan and one two-person plan when possible.
- Avoid treating reviewer reactions as statistically representative product evidence.

## Review Fit
- Useful: people who can react to clarity, trust, and decision confidence.
- Useful: people comfortable reviewing anonymized examples or their own local plan without sharing files.
- Not useful yet: requests for exact annual account instructions, advisor workflows, or tax-filing precision.

## Boundary
This sprint does not define a research panel, collect personal data, or broaden the product beyond consumer local-first planning.
