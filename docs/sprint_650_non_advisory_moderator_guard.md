# Sprint 650: Non-Advisory Moderator Guard

## Purpose
Keep outside-app review language calm, consumer-facing, and non-advisory.

## Moderator Guard
- Say "review evidence" instead of "recommendation" when discussing broad withdrawal families.
- Say "what seems clear or unclear" instead of "what should the user do."
- Do not tell a reviewer which account to draw from in a specific year.
- Do not present tax outcomes as tax advice.
- Do not imply that the product guarantees a retirement result.

## Outcome
- Manual reviews can test clarity without creating advice-like expectations.
- Reviewer notes stay aligned with product copy boundaries.

## Boundary
This sprint does not approve annual account-level sequencing, advisor tooling, or tax-advice language.
